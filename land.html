    Lamborghini

    A bold and innovative design that highlights the brand's high-performance cars.

Lamborghini is an Italian car manufacturer founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini. The company is named after its founder and is headquartered in Sant'Agata Bolognese, in Northern Italy.
The company has created a continuous series of dream cars, including the 350 GT, Miura, Espada, Countach, Diablo, and Murciélago, as well as limited editions such as the Reventón, Sesto Elemento, Veneno, and the Centenario.
In 2021, Lamborghini dedicated itself to the Countach for its 50th anniversary, releasing a limited and futuristic edition of the super sports car, the iconic Countach LPI 800-4, which boasts a revolutionary design and is a technology rule-breaker.
Lamborghini is a sole shareholder company part of the Audi Group and has its offices and location in Bologna, Italy.
